Metagistic

Metagistics is the disruptive cross-border global logistics company provides end-to-end supply chain services. Our company provides freight forwarding, custom clearance, cross-border e-commerce services and postal services.

Our company is a member of the British International Freight Association (BIFA) and the International Federation of Freight Forwarders Associations (FIATA). Since 2022 our company has been assessed and found to meet the requirements of ISO 9001:2015.

forwarder
